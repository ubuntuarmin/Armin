How Zelle payments work

We use Zelle because it’s fast and simple – but you never pay first.

  • 1 We agree on what we’re building and a clear price range for the project.
  • 2 We design and build your website, sharing progress and asking for your feedback.
  • 3 We go through revisions together until you say you’re 100% satisfied.
  • 4 Only then do you send the agreed payment via Zelle or another discussed way.

We will share our Zelle details with you directly in our text conversation when we’re ready for payment.

What working together feels like

  • ★ Clear communication – We explain things in simple language, step by step, and check in with you often.
  • ★ Unlimited revisions – We adjust layout, text, colors, and details until your site feels exactly right.
  • ★ Respect for your budget – We always suggest options that make sense for where you are now.
  • ★ No pressure, no rush to pay – Zelle payment only happens once you approve the final result.
