Our Mission

Building champions on and off the court since 2008

Our Vision

To create a legacy of athletic excellence, personal growth, and community impact that inspires the next generation of champions and leaders.

Our Journey

2008

The Beginning

Founded by Coach Mike Reynolds with just 12 players and a dream of building something special in our community.

2012

First Championship

Won our first regional championship, establishing Thunder Bolts as a competitive force in the league.

2016

New Home

Moved into Thunder Arena, our state-of-the-art training facility that supports player development at all levels.

2020

Youth Program Launch

Expanded our mission by launching community youth basketball programs, reaching over 500 young athletes.

2024

Record Season

Achieved our best season record and sent 3 players to professional leagues, proving our development model works.
