My Story

Business Strategist. Change Catalyst. Executive Coach.

Firoozeh Behnia is a senior business management consultant with over 18 years of experience helping organizations navigate complexity, lead transformation, and achieve sustainable growth. Her work spans strategic planning, organizational design, change management, and executive leadership development.

Born and educated in Tehran, Firoozeh pursued her postgraduate studies at INSEAD, one of the world's leading business schools, before building a distinguished career with global management consulting firms. She has led high-impact engagements across the financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, technology, and public sectors.

Firoozeh is known for combining rigorous analytical thinking with a deeply human approach to leadership — creating environments where executives and teams can do their best work and deliver results that endure long after the engagement ends.
