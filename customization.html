Customization

Make your site actually yours .

Our studio dosnt use pre-coded templates. we design and code everything from scratch based on your exact needs.

1. Theme & style

We will match your companys current theme and style or help you create one.

Colors

Let us know the colors that you want and dont want in your website. Or if you want gradient colors/ changing colors.

Theme

Let us know how you want customers to feel when visitng your site. For example: modern, simple, easy to use , advanced , animated.

Font size & animation

Tell us how big you want the text and how you want your headers to look. Also tell us about any animations you would like to include such as on scroll or on hover.

All of this is provided free of additional charges.

2. Your name

Choose where you want customers to see your site.

Sub Domain

If hosting your site with us, you can get a free sub domain from us like yoursite.armin.works, these are usally not recommended becuase they lower customer trust but are great for preview or just a buget option. All of these are free with some limits.

BYOD

If you have a domain alredy purchused our team can help guide you to connect it to your site. This is free of charge.

Get a domain

Our studio can find the best value domains and tranfer the bill to you. A .com domain will cost around $12-20 a year. Allowing us to buy it for you will streamline the procces and avoid techinical setup from your side.

3. How do you want your product?

Host your site on the web with us or take look at the other options.

Just the code

We will give you all the files we have coded for your site. examples: index.html, services.html, popup.js

Host with us

We will host your site for you and mange it for free. This has some limitations but is enough for small companys. We give you a choice of firebsase, github, netlify and cloudfare hosting as these are the most reliable and generous.

Premuim hosting

This is for companies that expect lots of users (more than 100 at once) and have huge viedos and files they want on their site. Cost is generally based on how much traffic your site gets. This is not reccomend at all for new companys.
