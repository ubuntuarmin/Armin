Frequently Asked Questions
Is the estimate free?
Yes — our on-site estimate visits are completely free with no obligation. We'll assess your project and provide a detailed written quote within 48 hours.
Is Total Power licensed and insured?
Absolutely. We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. Copies of our license and insurance certificates are available upon request.
How does the deposit work?
Your deposit is applied directly to the total project cost. It secures your place in our schedule and allows us to order materials.
What areas do you serve?
We serve the greater metro area and surrounding suburbs. Contact us to confirm availability for your specific location.
