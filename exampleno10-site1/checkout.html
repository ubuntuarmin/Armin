Frequently Asked Questions
What happens after I book?
You'll receive a confirmation email with a calendar invite and a brief pre-session questionnaire. Firoozeh reviews your context in advance to maximize session value.
Are sessions confidential?
Absolutely. All consultations are held under strict professional confidentiality. No details of your organization are shared with any third party.
Can I reschedule my session?
Yes — sessions can be rescheduled free of charge with 48 hours notice. Within 48 hours, a 25% reschedule fee applies. No-shows are non-refundable.
Is the Monthly Retainer a long-term commitment?
No lock-in required. The retainer is billed month-to-month and can be paused or cancelled with 30 days notice. Volume discounts apply for 3+ month commitments.
