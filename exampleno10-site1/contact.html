Frequently Asked Questions

How does a typical engagement begin? +

Every engagement starts with a complimentary 30-minute discovery call where we discuss your organization's challenges, goals, and context. If there's a mutual fit, I'll then prepare a tailored proposal outlining scope, methodology, timeline, and fees. There's no obligation at any stage.

Do you work with organizations outside of Canada? +

Absolutely. I work with organizations across North America, Europe, the Middle East, and beyond. Engagements can be conducted remotely, on-site, or in a hybrid format depending on your needs and the nature of the work. I have delivered projects in 12 countries to date.

What industries do you have experience in? +

My experience spans financial services, healthcare, manufacturing, technology, retail, public sector, energy, education, and telecommunications. I bring both deep functional expertise and the ability to apply cross-industry insights that drive innovation within your sector.

How long does a typical consulting engagement last? +

Engagement length varies significantly by scope and complexity. Advisory retainers are ongoing (minimum 3 months). Project-based engagements typically range from 3 months for focused strategy work to 18–24 months for enterprise-wide transformation programs. I always work to define the shortest path to your desired outcomes.

Do you work with smaller businesses and startups? +

Yes. While much of my portfolio involves large and mid-sized organizations, I also work selectively with ambitious small businesses and high-growth startups — particularly those at an inflection point where clear strategy, better operations, or stronger leadership are critical to the next stage of growth.

Are consultations confidential? +

Absolute confidentiality is a cornerstone of my practice. All discussions, documents, and information shared during any engagement are treated as strictly confidential. I sign NDAs at the start of every engagement upon request and never share client-specific information without explicit permission.
