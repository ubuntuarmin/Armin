Common Questions

Delivery FAQs

Is delivery really free? No minimums, no fees? +
Yes — completely free. No minimum order amount, no delivery fee, no tip required (though always appreciated!). We believe the price you see is the price you pay. Free delivery in Katy is part of our commitment to the community, not a promotion with an expiration date.
How quickly will I receive my order? +
Standard delivery is within 24–48 hours of placing your order. Many orders placed before noon on weekdays are delivered the same day or next morning. We'll confirm your delivery window by text after your order is placed.
What if I'm not home during delivery? +
Not a problem. Your order comes in an insulated cooler bag with enough ice packs to keep it fresh for up to 4 hours after delivery. We'll leave it at your door and send you a text when it arrives. If your porch is exposed to extreme heat, let us know and we'll arrange a time when you're home.
Can I set up a recurring weekly delivery? +
Absolutely. We can set up a weekly or bi-weekly recurring delivery on your preferred day. Just mention it in your order notes or text us after your first order. We'll lock in your schedule and reach out before each delivery to confirm your menu preferences.
What if something is wrong with my order? +
Text or call us immediately at (832) 555-0194. We stand behind every order, and if something isn't right — wrong recipe, quality concern, anything — we'll replace or refund it, no questions asked. We'd rather lose a sale than lose your trust.
