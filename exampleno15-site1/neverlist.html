Them vs. Us

Every row below is a real ingredient commonly found in "premium" pet food. Here's what we use instead — and why.

🚫 What "They" Use
✅ What We Use
💀
Chicken By-Product Meal
Rendered remains including feet, heads, intestines. "Meal" = dried at high heat destroying most nutrients.
→
🐔
Whole Free-Range Chicken
Named, real breast meat from cage-free, humanely raised chickens. You can see it. You can recognize it.
🧪
BHA / BHT Preservatives
Synthetic chemical preservatives. BHA is listed as a possible carcinogen by the National Institutes of Health.
→
❄️
Refrigeration & Correct Portioning
We preserve freshness the natural way — cold storage and made-to-order preparation. No chemicals needed.
🎨
Red Dye 40 / Yellow 5
Synthetic petroleum-derived dyes. Dogs are colorblind to red. These dyes exist to fool you — not your dog.
→
🌿
Natural Color from Real Ingredients
Sweet potato turns it orange. Kale adds green. Salmon gives pink. Real color from real food. Always.
⚗️
Propylene Glycol
An anti-freeze compound used in semi-moist pet foods to retain moisture. Linked to Heinz body anemia in cats — and its safety in dogs is debated.
→
🦴
In-House Bone Broth
We add moisture the delicious way — slow-simmered bone broth that also adds collagen and joint-supporting compounds.
🌽
Corn / Wheat / Soy Fillers
Cheap caloric fillers that bulk up food without meaningful nutrition. Common allergens for dogs. Often GMO.
→
🍠
Sweet Potato & Brown Rice
When we use carbohydrates, they're purposeful — sweet potato for fiber and vitamins, brown rice for digestibility.
🏭
Meat & Bone Meal
A rendered product from mammal tissues including bones. Can come from "4D animals" — dead, dying, diseased, or disabled.
→
🥩
Grass-Fed Whole Beef
Real, traceable beef from local farms. Antibiotic-free, hormone-free, humanely raised. You'd buy it at the store.
💊
Synthetic Vitamin Premix
Lab-created vitamin blends added to compensate for nutrients destroyed during ultra-high-heat processing.
→
🥦
Whole Food Nutrition
We cook at lower temperatures to preserve natural vitamins. Kale, broccoli, sweet potato, and eggs provide complete nutrition — no lab required.
🐷
"Animal Digest"
A palatability enhancer made by chemically hydrolysing animal tissue. Not required to be from a specific animal or quality standard.
→
🫙
Real Aromatics & Broth
We make food that tastes good because it is good. Slow-cooked proteins and vegetables create natural, irresistible flavor dogs love.
