CARE GUIDE

A stronger product experience includes after-purchase guidance. This page gives the Two Wheel example a more complete and more professional customer journey by covering leather, fragrance, apparel, and accessories in one place.

Back to shopReturns policy

Leather Jackets

Condition the shell lightly two or three times a season, keep jackets on wide hangers, and avoid storing them in plastic garment bags.

  • Spot clean first before reaching for full cleaners.
  • Let wet leather air dry naturally away from direct heat.
  • Use the included dust bag or a breathable cotton cover between wears.

Fragrance

Store bottles upright, away from bathroom humidity and direct sunlight. Spray onto pulse points or the inside lining of a jacket for a longer scent trail.

  • Keep caps tightly secured to reduce oxidation.
  • Use smaller bottles for travel instead of decanting from the full size.
  • Apply one or two sprays first, then build slowly if needed.

Heavyweight Cotton & Fleece

Wash tees, hoodies, and knitwear cold and inside out. Low heat or line drying will help preserve print clarity, fleece loft, and shape.

  • Avoid over-drying heavyweight cotton.
  • Reshape waffle or knit items while damp.
  • Do not iron directly over prints, puff ink, or coated trims.

Accessories & Bags

For hats, bags, jewelry, and eyewear, use a microfiber cloth or damp soft cloth first. Store smaller accessories in pouches or cases to reduce scratches.

  • Keep metal jewelry dry between wears.
  • Use the hard case for sunglasses during travel.
  • Do not overload structured tote or sling silhouettes if you want them to hold their shape.
