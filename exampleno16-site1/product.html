Phantom Rider is the signature fragrance from Two Wheel Clothing. Built for riders who demand presence before they even start the engine. This Eau de Toilette opens with a burst of citrus and black pepper, then settles into a deep, smoky heart of vetiver, tobacco, and cedarwood. The drydown is warm musk with a whisper of leather — as close to the open road as a bottle can get.

Created in collaboration with a master perfumer in Grasse, France, Phantom Rider was designed from the ground up to complement the lifestyle of a modern rider. It works on the bike, in the bar, at the meeting, and everywhere in between. Long-lasting and projection-heavy — you'll be remembered long after you leave the room.

Customer Questions & Answers

Q How long does the scent last on skin? ▼
A In our testing and based on hundreds of customer reviews, Phantom Rider EDT typically lasts 8–12 hours on skin. Results vary by skin type — drier skin may need a single re-application after 6–7 hours. Applying to pulse points (wrists, neck, chest) dramatically improves longevity.
Answered by Two Wheel Clothing · November 2, 2024
Q Is this suitable for warm weather or is it a heavy winter scent? ▼
A Phantom Rider is technically a year-round fragrance but performs best in cooler temperatures — fall, winter, and cool spring evenings. In summer heat, use 1–2 sprays only. The tobacco and vetiver heart notes can become very intense in high humidity. For summer, we recommend Chrome Dusk EDT, which is lighter and more citrus-forward.
Answered by Two Wheel Clothing · October 18, 2024
Q What's the difference between the 30ml, 50ml and 100ml? Same formula? ▼
A Yes — exact same EDT formula across all sizes. The 30ml is ideal for travel or trying the scent. The 50ml is the most popular size for everyday use. The 100ml offers the best value per ml and is recommended if you know you love it. All three come in the same frosted glass bottle, just different heights.
Answered by Two Wheel Clothing · October 5, 2024
Q Does this ship internationally? How long does delivery take? ▼
A Yes, we ship to 42+ countries. Standard domestic delivery is 3–5 business days. Express is 1–2 days. International shipping typically takes 7–14 business days depending on destination and customs. Free shipping is available on US orders over $75. International orders over $120 qualify for discounted shipping. All orders are tracked.
Answered by Two Wheel Clothing · September 22, 2024
Q Is this scent more masculine, feminine, or unisex? ▼
A Phantom Rider was designed as a unisex fragrance, though it leans slightly masculine due to the tobacco and vetiver base. About 68% of our customers who purchased this identify as male, 32% female or non-binary. Many customers in the female demographic say they appreciate it for its unconventional boldness. Fragrance is personal — we encourage everyone to try it.
