Returns & Exchanges

If your order isn’t the right fit, we make returns and exchanges clear and straightforward.

Return Window

  • Returns are accepted within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached.
  • Fragrance products must be unopened and sealed to qualify for return.

Exchanges

Need a different size? We support one complimentary exchange per order for eligible apparel, based on available stock.

How to Start a Return

  • Contact support with your order number and reason for return.
  • You’ll receive return instructions and a return authorization.
  • Refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days after inspection.

Non-Returnable Items

Final sale items, gift cards, and worn or damaged items that do not meet policy standards are not eligible for return.

Questions? Contact support.
