Shipping Information

We ship quickly and provide reliable tracking so you can follow every order from checkout to delivery.

Processing Times

  • Orders placed before 2:00 PM are processed the same business day.
  • Standard processing is 1–2 business days during high-volume periods.
  • You’ll receive an email confirmation once your order ships.

Delivery Speeds

  • Standard Shipping: 3–5 business days.
  • Express Shipping: 1–2 business days.
  • Free standard shipping on U.S. orders over $75.

Shipping Carriers

We partner with trusted carriers including USPS, UPS, and FedEx depending on destination and service level selected at checkout.

International Orders

Delivery windows vary by region. Duties and import fees (if applicable) are charged by local customs authorities and are not included in product prices.
