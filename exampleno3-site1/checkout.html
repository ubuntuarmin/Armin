Frequently Asked Questions
How fresh is the bread?
All breads are baked fresh daily in our stone ovens using the same authentic methods brought from Iran. We never sell day-old bread.
Can I place a large catering order?
Absolutely! Select the Custom Catering Box or leave details in the notes. For orders over 20 pieces, please call us 48 hours in advance.
Do you offer gluten-free options?
Currently our breads contain wheat flour. Please note dietary needs in your order and we'll advise on the best options available.
What is your refund policy?
Due to the perishable nature of our products, refunds are not offered once an order is baked. Please call us within 2 hours of pickup for any quality concerns.
