Our Story

A family's love for sweets, turned into something beautiful

How It All Began

Born from a Grandmother's Kitchen

Sweet Bliss started in 2016 as a small project — a young boy's dream of becoming a chef and his mother's way of keeping grandma's beloved dessert recipes alive. What began with a hand mixer, a tiny kitchen, and an enormous love for sugar has grown into a full-time family business.

Two years after our first sale, we took the leap full time. Today we pour that same passion into every macaron, every custom cake, and every cup of coffee we serve. We're not just a bakery — we're a gathering place where memories are made over warm soup and sweet things.

Our café blends a European patisserie tradition with the comfort of a neighbourhood hangout. Whether you're here for a perfectly cooked chicken parmesan, a nutella crepe, or a custom wedding cake, you'll always find warmth, quality, and a friendly face.
