Frequently Asked Questions
How far in advance should I order?
Custom cakes require at least 5–7 days notice. Pastry boxes and macaron boxes need 48 hours. Catering packages require 2 weeks advance booking.
Do you offer gluten-free or nut-free options?
Yes! We can accommodate many dietary needs. Please note your requirements in the design notes and we'll confirm availability.
Can I book this as a gift?
Absolutely! We offer gift wrapping and can include a handwritten note. Just mention it in the special notes field and we'll take care of the rest.
What is your cancellation policy?
Full refund if cancelled 72+ hours before pickup. 50% refund within 72 hours. No refund within 24 hours as preparation will have begun.
