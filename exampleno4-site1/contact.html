Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How far in advance do I need to order a custom cake?

We require at least 48 hours for standard custom cakes. For wedding cakes and large multi-tier orders, we recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance to secure your date.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We offer local delivery within Houston. Delivery fees vary by distance. For catering orders, delivery and setup is included in all packages. Call us to check your area.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

We do our best to accommodate gluten-free, dairy-free, and nut-free requests. Please let us know about any allergies when placing your order so we can advise accordingly.

Do you offer tastings for wedding cakes?

Absolutely! We offer complimentary tasting sessions for wedding cake enquiries. Contact us to schedule a tasting appointment with one of our bakers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, cash, and Venmo. A 50% deposit is required for all custom orders, with the balance due upon collection or delivery.

Is parking available?

Yes — we have free on-site parking available for all guests. There is also additional street parking available on Sugar Lane.
