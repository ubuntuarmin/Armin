Who We Are

Family-Owned, Community-Focused

ProCraft Remodeling is a family-owned and operated general contracting business with over 25 years of experience serving the greater Houston area. Founded on the belief that every homeowner deserves access to high-quality renovations at fair prices, we have grown from a small local operation to a trusted name throughout Sugar Land, Katy, Kemah, and surrounding communities.

Our founder started this business with a simple mission: deliver honest work, quality results, and treat every client's home like our own. That mission still guides us today, in every tile laid, every wall painted, and every bathroom transformed.

We are fully licensed and insured, and we take pride in transparent communication throughout every project — from the initial free consultation to the final walkthrough.

Our Journey

Built Over 25 Years

Late 1990s

Founded in Houston

Started as a small crew specializing in kitchen and bathroom remodeling. Word-of-mouth reputation grew quickly through honest, quality work.

2005

Expanded to Full Remodeling Services

Added flooring, painting, drywall, and structural services — becoming a full-service general contractor for the Houston area.

2012

Grew to Multiple Service Areas

Expanded service coverage to Sugar Land, Katy, Kemah, and surrounding communities. Established direct supplier relationships for better pricing.

2018

Commercial & Multifamily Services Added

Began serving commercial clients and multifamily property owners, offering the same high standards we bring to residential work.

Today

500+ Projects Completed & Counting

Proud to have transformed hundreds of homes and businesses, maintaining a 5-star reputation built entirely on client satisfaction.
