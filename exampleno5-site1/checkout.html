Frequently Asked Questions
Is the estimate really free?
Yes — your on-site estimate visit is completely free. The deposit secures your scheduling slot and is fully applied to your project total.
Are you licensed and insured?
Absolutely. ProCraft Remodeling is fully licensed and insured in Texas. We carry general liability and workers' comp insurance on every job.
How long do projects typically take?
Kitchen remodels: 3–6 weeks. Bathrooms: 2–4 weeks. Flooring: 1–5 days depending on square footage. We'll give you a firm timeline after our estimate visit.
Do you offer warranties on your work?
Yes — all workmanship is backed by our 2-year labor warranty. Manufacturer warranties on materials are passed through to you in full.
