Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free estimates? ▼
Yes! We offer completely free in-home consultations and detailed written construction proposals at no cost to you. There's never any obligation to proceed.
How long does a typical remodel take? ▼
Project timelines vary depending on scope. A bathroom remodel typically takes 5–14 days, kitchen remodels 2–4 weeks, and full home renovations 4–10 weeks. We'll give you a specific timeline in your written proposal.
Are you licensed and insured? ▼
Absolutely. We are fully licensed as a general contractor in Texas and carry comprehensive general liability and workers' compensation insurance to protect you throughout the project.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept personal checks and all major credit cards. Payment schedules are outlined clearly in your written proposal — typically a deposit upfront and final payment on completion.
Do you handle permits? ▼
Yes, when permits are required for your project, we handle obtaining them on your behalf as part of our full-service approach. We ensure all work meets local building codes and passes inspection.
Can you help with material selection? ▼
Yes! We work directly with major suppliers including Arizona Tile, Interceramic, Floor & Decor, Home Depot, Lowe's, and more. Our design consultant can guide you through options, and the customer always has the final say on products and providers.
