FAQ

Common Questions

Do you offer dairy-free and vegan options?

Absolutely! We offer a rotating selection of dairy-free sorbets and oat-milk based ice creams. Items clearly marked with 🟢 on our menu board are vegan-friendly. Ask our staff for daily availability.

Can I order a custom ice cream cake?

Yes! We make beautiful custom ice cream cakes. We need at least 48 hours notice. You can pick any combination of our flavors, and our pastry team will decorate it to your specifications. Contact us or visit in person to place a cake order.

Do you do catering for events?

We love catering events! From small birthday parties to large corporate functions, we bring our ice cream (and sometimes our scoop cart) to you. Minimum of 25 servings for catering orders. Get in touch at least one week in advance.

How often do you add new flavors?

We introduce seasonal flavors every quarter and add a "Chef's Surprise" limited edition every Friday. Follow us on Instagram for first announcements — new flavors often sell out the same day!

Is there seating available?

We have 20 indoor seats and a lovely outdoor patio with 16 additional seats (weather permitting). The patio is dog-friendly! We're also steps from Riverside Park if you want to enjoy your scoop in the sunshine.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! Physical gift cards are available in-store in any amount starting from $10. They make the sweetest gifts (pun intended) and never expire. We're working on digital gift cards — stay tuned!
