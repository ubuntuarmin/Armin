Our Story

Made with Love &
Real Ingredients

Since 2018, Scoops & Dreams has been crafting small-batch artisan ice cream from scratch. We source our dairy from a family farm just 30 miles away, and every flavor is developed by our head ice cream artisan who spent years training in Italy and Japan.

🥛
Farm-Fresh Dairy
Cream from grass-fed cows at Meadowbrook Farm, delivered fresh every morning.
🌱
No Artificial Anything
Zero artificial colors, flavors, or preservatives — ever. Real food, real taste.
🎨
New Flavors Every Season
We create 10+ new seasonal flavors every quarter inspired by local harvests and global cuisines.
