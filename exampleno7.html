6-Page Website

Beyond the Canvas Art Studio

A creative space for all ages to explore and grow through Art

🖌️ ✨ 🎨

A beautifully crafted 6-page website for "Beyond the Canvas Art Studio" — an art studio by Farnaz Amin offering oil painting, drawing, crafts, resin art, fine Art classes, and hands-on art camps. The design uses a warm terracotta and gold palette on rich charcoal backgrounds to evoke creativity and craft. It's elegant, gallery-inspired, and fully responsive — built to connect students with Farnaz's inspiring classes.

🏠 Home 🎨 Classes 🖼️ Gallery 🛒 Book Now (Checkout) 👩‍🎨 Biography 📋 Contact
🎨

This template is designed for art studios, private art classes, galleries, and creative studios. It includes three service sections with modal popups, a gallery of works, biography of the artist, private events, birthday parties, and a contact form — everything a real art studio needs online.

  • Animated hero with floating paint canvas, brushstroke effects, and artist stats — makes a stunning first impression
  • Three service sections: Private Events & Classes, Special Party & Meeting, Training Painting & Drawing — each with detailed modal popups
  • Stripe-ready Checkout / Book Now page — class picker, quantity control, order summary, date/time selection, customer form, Stripe Elements placeholder with integration comments
  • Modern design with Google Fonts (Inter + Playfair Display), glassmorphism nav, pill-shaped CTA buttons, and polished form inputs
  • Meet Farnaz Amin biography section with artistic achievements and stats
  • Portfolio gallery with mosaic grid layout and interactive lightbox viewer
  • Full biography page with timeline, values, and studio information
  • Scrolling marquee, scroll-reveal animations, and hover effects throughout all pages
  • Fully responsive with mobile hamburger menu — consistent across all 6 pages
