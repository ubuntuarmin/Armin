Precision Mechanics

Movement With
Intention

NEXUS actuators are engineered for the full dynamic range of real-world interaction — from the delicate manipulation of fragile objects to the powerful exertion required for heavy industrial tasks. Each joint integrates position sensing, torque measurement, and thermal management into a single compact module, achieving a transparency that rivals biological muscle.

Our proprietary elastic actuator design provides passive compliance, making every NEXUS robot inherently safe to work alongside humans while delivering force-control precision at the sub-Newton level. This isn't servo-based motion — it's mechanical intelligence.

03
Sensor Array

Perception Beyond
Human Limits

The NEXUS Sensor Fusion System combines high-resolution solid-state LiDAR, stereo RGB-D cameras, inertial measurement, tactile sensor arrays, and acoustic proximity detection into a single unified perceptual model. The robot doesn't see the world in layers — it experiences it as a seamlessly integrated whole.

Our sensor fusion algorithm operates at 200Hz, continuously building and refining a probabilistic 3D map of the environment with centimeter accuracy. Whether navigating a cluttered factory floor or assisting in a hospital corridor, NEXUS robots always know precisely where they are, what surrounds them, and what is about to change.

04
Energy Systems

Power Without
Compromise

Autonomous operation demands energy systems that match the ambition of the mission. NEXUS developed a high-density lithium-silicon battery architecture that achieves 40% greater energy density than conventional lithium-ion at the same mass. Combined with a regenerative energy recovery system on every joint, NEXUS robots recapture kinetic energy on every step, every movement, every deceleration.

Thermal management is intelligent and predictive — the onboard system models heat generation across all components and dynamically adjusts performance thresholds to maintain safe operating temperatures in environments ranging from Arctic cold to desert heat, without interrupting mission continuity.
