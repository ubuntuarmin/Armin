A cinematic, production-ready 5-page website for NEXUS ROBOTICS — a hyper-premium futuristic robotics company. Built with Three.js for real-time 3D, GSAP ScrollTrigger for scroll-driven animations, and Apple-level visual design. The site features a 3D humanoid robot hero, interactive product gallery, neural-network technology visualization, animated brand timeline, and a rotating globe contact page. Every pixel is crafted with film-grade precision and emotional storytelling.

🏠 Home 🤖 Products ⚙️ Technology 🌐 About 📡 Contact
🎬

This template is designed for robotics companies, AI startups, advanced manufacturing firms, and deep-tech brands. It includes a Three.js humanoid robot hero, 3D product modals, neural-network animations, an animated company timeline, and a rotating wireframe globe — all with cinematic camera motion and hyper-realistic PBR materials.

  • Cinematic Three.js hero: 15-part humanoid robot with emissive materials, 1,200 orbiting particles, volumetric lighting, and mouse-reactive rotation
  • Products page with 3 interactive 3D robot models (NX-7 Humanoid, RA-Pro Arm, AX-Nano Drone), full-screen modal reveals, and animated spec sheets
  • Technology page with scroll-driven neural network (55 animated nodes + pulsing connection lines), four engineering deep-dive sections
  • About page with parallax hero, animated milestone timeline (2013–2025), 4-person founding team, and cinematic mission statement
  • Contact page with rotating Three.js wireframe globe, 4 global office markers, tactile form micro-interactions, and success particle animation
  • Magnetic custom cursor with smooth follower lag across all 5 pages
  • Film-grain texture overlay and chromatic-aberration-inspired aesthetic throughout
  • GSAP ScrollTrigger animations: scroll-driven fades, slides, stagger reveals, and counter animations
  • Fully responsive with mobile hamburger menu, consistent dark space theme (#050A14/#0A84FF/#00D4FF)
  • No external assets — all 3D geometry is procedurally generated with Three.js BufferGeometry
