Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

We recommend contacting us at least 4 weeks in advance for custom wedding cakes and at least 2 weeks for celebration cakes. For large orders, 6 weeks is preferred during peak season (May–June, November–December).

Do you offer gluten-free or vegan options?

Yes — we have a small but growing selection of gluten-free pastries and fully plant-based items. Please let us know your dietary requirements when ordering and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Can I book a baking class as a gift?

Absolutely. We offer gift vouchers for all our workshops and baking classes. Simply contact us by email or in the form above and we'll send you a beautiful printed or digital gift certificate within 24 hours.

What is your delivery radius for catering?

We deliver within central Paris (arrondissements 1–20) and the inner suburbs (Île-de-France). For events beyond this zone, please contact us directly — we regularly travel for weddings and large events across France.
