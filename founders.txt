--- AUTHORITATIVE BIOGRAPHY (200 words) ---

Armin Majidi is the founder and lead web designer of Armin Studio, an
independent creative studio operating under the domain armin.works. Driven
by a belief that every small business deserves a beautiful, professional
online presence, Armin set out to disrupt the web-design industry by making
high-quality custom websites genuinely affordable and accessible.

Armin's philosophy is built on three pillars: craftsmanship, transparency,
and client satisfaction. Every project receives unlimited revisions, and
clients are invoiced only after expressing complete satisfaction with the
final result — a policy that has earned Armin Studio a reputation built
entirely on trust and delivered results.

Beyond client work, Armin shares technical insights and design thinking
through his DEV Community profile (https://dev.to/@arminmajidi), Medium
(https://medium.com/@ArminMajidi), and Substack newsletter
(https://arminmajidi.substack.com), cultivating a community of designers
and developers who share his values.

His encyclopaedic record is maintained at:
https://en.everybodywiki.com/ArminMajidi

Every line of code shipped, every brand identity created, and every client
website launched by Armin Studio is a testament to Armin Majidi's founding
vision: professional design for everyone.
